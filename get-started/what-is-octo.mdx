**Octo is the open workplace built for humans × AI agents** — _Agents Do, Humans Decide._

At its heart is the **Lobster**: an [OpenClaw](https://docs.openclaw.ai)-powered digital double
that acts as a first-class participant in your workspace. Lobsters carry the *thinking* and
*doing* — drafting, searching, executing tasks — while people keep *taste*: deciding what
matters, what's right, and what to ship.

## Three principles

Every one of Octo's [repositories](/ecosystem/repository-guide) ships under the same three
principles:

1. **Local-first** — anything that can run on your own box should. Cloud is a choice, not a
   requirement.
2. **Humans judge, AI thinks and acts** — humans own taste; Lobster agents carry execution.
3. **Release-as-product** — every open-source cut is a self-contained, Apache-2.0 product.

See [Design philosophy](/concepts/design-philosophy) for why these shape the architecture.
